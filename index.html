TimTV is a media streaming service running on a Jellyfin server, mimicing the interface of Netflix or Hulu. For media requests, please reach out to me on Discord. Enjoy!

Configuration

TimTV can be accessed via a web browser from any device. Most smartphones and TV streaming devices have the app Jellyfin, which I reccomend for streaming unless you are watching from a desktop. For privacy, your device must be connected to a private network thru a NordVPN service called Meshnet.

To get started, download NordVPN on your computer or smartphone. No subscription is necessary.

Follow the Meshnet invitation in the email from NordVPN to connect to the private server. NordVPN must be downloaded on the device you are streaming from, including TVs. Reach out to me if you have any issues.

Finally, within your web browser or Jellyfin app, paste the link below to sign into TimTV. Message me for login info.

http://TimTV.nord:8096/
